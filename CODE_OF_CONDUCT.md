# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, constructive, and easy to work with.

We're here to build something fun and useful. That only works if people feel welcome contributing, asking questions, and sharing ideas.

## Expected behavior

- be kind
- be patient
- give clear feedback
- assume good intent
- keep disagreements technical, not personal

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment
- insults
- discrimination
- bad-faith trolling
- deliberately hostile conduct

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that make the space worse for everyone else.
